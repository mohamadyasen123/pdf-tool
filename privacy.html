PDF Masterly

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 22, 2025

Privacy First: PDF Masterly is built with your privacy as our top priority. All file processing happens directly in your web browser using JavaScript. Your files never leave your device and are never uploaded to our servers.

1. Introduction

Welcome to PDF Masterly. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our website and services.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

PDF Masterly does NOT collect any personal information such as:

  • Names, email addresses, or phone numbers
  • Account credentials (we don't require registration)
  • Payment information (our service is completely free)
  • Your uploaded files or document content

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

We may automatically collect certain technical information when you visit our website:

  • Browser Information: Browser type, version, and language
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and screen resolution
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent on site, and referral source
  • IP Address: For security and analytics purposes (anonymized)

2.3 Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to improve your experience:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for website functionality
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors use our site (Google Analytics may be used)
  • Advertising Cookies: May be used if we implement Google AdSense in the future

3. How We Use Your Information

The limited information we collect is used only for:

  • Improving website performance and user experience
  • Understanding usage patterns and popular features
  • Detecting and preventing technical issues or abuse
  • Complying with legal obligations

4. Client-Side Processing

Your Files Stay Private:

  • All PDF conversions, merging, compression, and other operations happen entirely in your browser
  • Files are processed using JavaScript libraries (PDF.js and jsPDF)
  • Your documents never leave your device
  • We have no access to your file content
  • Files are not stored on our servers because they never reach them

5. Third-Party Services

We may use the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Cloudflare CDN: For hosting JavaScript libraries (PDF.js, jsPDF)
  • Google Analytics (if implemented): For website analytics
  • Google AdSense (if implemented): For displaying advertisements

These services may collect information as described in their respective privacy policies. We recommend reviewing their policies:

  • Cloudflare Privacy Policy: https://www.cloudflare.com/privacypolicy/
  • Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

6. Data Security

We take data security seriously:

  • HTTPS Encryption: Our website uses SSL/TLS encryption
  • No File Storage: Since files are processed client-side, there's no risk of server-side data breaches
  • Regular Updates: We keep our security practices up to date

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: Request information about data we hold (minimal, as described above)
  • Right to Deletion: Request deletion of your data (limited to cookies/browsing data)
  • Right to Opt-Out: Disable cookies in your browser settings
  • Right to Object: Object to data processing for marketing purposes

8. Children's Privacy

PDF Masterly is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

9. International Users

PDF Masterly is accessible worldwide. If you are accessing our service from outside your country, please note that your information may be processed in the country where our servers are located. By using our service, you consent to such processing.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

11. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have specific rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to delete personal information
  • Right to opt-out of the sale of personal information

Note: PDF Masterly does not sell personal information.

12. GDPR Compliance (European Users)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), we comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). We process your data based on:

  • Legitimate Interest: To operate and improve our website
  • Consent: For cookies and analytics (where applicable)

13. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

Email: privacy@pdfmasterly.com
